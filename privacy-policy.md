# Privacy Policy — TinyBrainPlay

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## 1. Introduction

TinyBrainPlay ("the App") is an educational game designed for 
children aged 3–8. We are committed to protecting the privacy 
of all users, especially children.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

TinyBrainPlay does NOT collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Photos, camera, or microphone data
- Any data from children under 13

## 3. Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data ONLY on your device 
(using AsyncStorage):
- Game scores and progress
- Coin and XP totals
- Daily streak count
- Badges and collectibles earned
- Sound on/off preference

This data NEVER leaves your device. We have no access to it.

## 4. No Internet Required

TinyBrainPlay works completely offline. The App does not 
make any network requests or connect to any server.

## 5. No Advertising

The App contains NO advertisements and NO third-party 
advertising SDKs.

## 6. No In-App Purchases

The App is completely free with NO in-app purchases.

## 7. Third-Party Services

We do NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, 
or tracking services.

## 8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

TinyBrainPlay is designed for children under 13. In 
accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection 
Act (COPPA):
- We do not knowingly collect personal information 
  from children
- No account creation is required
- No parental consent is needed as no data is collected

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the 
"Last Updated" date above.
